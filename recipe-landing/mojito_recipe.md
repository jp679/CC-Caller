# Mojito Recipe

## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz white rum
- 1 oz fresh lime juice (about half a lime)
- 2 tsp sugar (or 0.75 oz simple syrup)
- 6–8 fresh mint leaves
- Club soda
- Ice

## Preparation
1. Place mint leaves and sugar in the bottom of a glass.
2. Muddle gently — press and twist to release the mint oils, don't shred the leaves.
3. Squeeze in the lime juice.
4. Add the rum and stir to dissolve the sugar.
5. Fill the glass with ice.
6. Top with club soda and stir gently to combine.
7. Garnish with a sprig of mint and a lime wheel.

## Tips
- Use fresh spearmint for the best flavor.
- Muddle lightly — bruising the mint too hard releases bitter chlorophyll.
- Crushed ice keeps it colder longer.
